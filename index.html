May 2020 – June 2020 • ACE Lab

An internal app for research purposes to conduct a 30-day study on hypertensive patients. The app guides them along the study with simple and minimal features which educate the user on hypertension and allow the user to view and track related health data.

August 2020 – Current • ACE Lab

A diabetes management app for underserved communities with limited health resources. The app lets users track blood glucose, food intake, exercise, weight, and medication through in-the-works wireless technology that will be provided to patients.

about

Hello! My name is Felicia Invedy, and I'm currently a fourth-year Computer Science and Statistics student at Texas A&M University from Sugar Land, TX! I'm also pursuing minors in Art (New Media) and Game Design & Development. Some topics of or relating to computer science that interest me include HCI (UI/UX), computer graphics, game development, and data visualization.

I'm a UI/UX designer for ACE Labs here at A&M and a design director for TAMU Datathon 2020! I'm also the design officer for both Aggie Women in Computer Science (AWiCS) and tidaltamu (Tethered Informatics Data Analytics Lab).

In my free time, I love drawing, designing, and playing video games! I listen to classical music and video game scores, and am also involved in the university orchestra as a cellist.
