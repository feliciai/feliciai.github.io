Hello! My name is Felicia Invedy, and I'm currently a fourth-year Computer Science and Statistics student at Texas A&M University from Sugar Land, TX! I'm also pursuing minors in Art (New Media) and Game Design & Development. Some topics of or relating to computer science that interest me include HCI (UI/UX), computer graphics, game design, and data visualization.

I'm a UI/UX designer for ACE Labs here at A&M and a design director for TAMU Datathon 2020 and 2021! I'm also the design & branding officer for Aggie Women in Computer Science (AWiCS) and webmaster for tidaltamu (Tethered Informatics Data Analytics Lab).

I'm passionate about helping others and creating an environment for them to learn, grow, and challenge themselves! I like being "behind the scenes" and seeing others' success through the impact of my work and efforts. I currently volunteer as a designer for Develop For Good, an intercollegiate technology and design for social good organization.

In my free time, I love drawing, designing, and playing video games! I also love to listen to classical music and video game scores, and am also involved in the university orchestra as a cellist.
